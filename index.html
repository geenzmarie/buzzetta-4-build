Joy in the Mundane

There are so many small moments where we can find joy to fill up our tanks when we feel drained from the heaviness of the world or exhausted from our responsibilites. While there are large joys like vacations, or new expensive items - I find that the small every day items do wonders for the soul as well. These moments and objects may seem simple to others, but they fill up my cup and give me energy to get through the dreary days.

Items that Bring Me Joy

  1. My mini dachshund, Miles, and his big puppy dog eyes and his grinch toes
  2. The warmth of the sunshine on my skin or how it looks peeking through the trees
  3. A good cup of coffee or a fresh cappuccino
  4. Taking a walk through the park and hearing the birds sing
  5. Feeling the fresh breeze coming in through the windows
  6. The smallest flowers climbing through the cracks and reaching towards the sun

How to Find Small Joys

Tiny joys are all around us. I advice others to listen to their inner child that still exists within them, and remember how fascinated we were with the little things. Slow down and be here now. The past is behind us, the future is not guaranteed, but we can be in the present moment and see what joys we can find today.
